Cybersecurity Basics

Understanding Digital Security

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. These attacks aim to steal information, disrupt operations, or cause financial damage.

Why Cybersecurity Matters

As technology becomes an essential part of daily life, cybersecurity is critical for protecting personal information, businesses, and national infrastructure.

  • Malware: Viruses, ransomware, spyware
  • Phishing: Fraudulent emails and websites
  • Social Engineering: Manipulating users
  • Denial of Service: Overloading systems
